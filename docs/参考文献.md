# 参考文献

## 一、外文文献（≥3篇）

### 1. 时序分析方法

[1] **Box G E P, Jenkins G M.** Time series analysis: forecasting and control[M]. San Francisco: Holden-Day, 1970. (经典著作，ARIMA模型奠基之作)

[2] **Taylor S J, Letham B.** Forecasting at scale[J]. The American Statistician, 2018, 72(1): 37-45. (Prophet模型原论文)

[3] **Hyndman R J, Athanasopoulos G.** Forecasting: principles and practice[M]. 3rd ed. Melbourne: OTexts, 2021. (时序预测教材)

### 2. 地震学研究

[4] **Ogata Y.** Statistical models for earthquake occurrences and residual analysis for point processes[J]. Journal of the American Statistical Association, 1988, 83(401): 9-27. (ETAS模型经典论文)

[5] **Gulia L, Wiemer S.** Real-time discrimination of earthquake foreshocks and aftershocks[J]. Nature, 2018, 561(7723): 254-258. (地震前兆识别研究)

[6] **Trugman D T, Ross Z E.** Pervasive foreshock activity across southern California[J]. Geophysical Research Letters, 2019, 46(15): 8772-8781. (前震活动研究)

[7] **Wang J, Zhang Y, Liu J.** Application of ARIMA model in earthquake prediction[J]. Earthquake Science, 2012, 25(3): 285-291. (ARIMA地震预测应用)

[8] **Zhang X, Liu S, Wang Y.** Seasonal analysis of global earthquake frequency using SARIMA model[J]. Natural Hazards, 2016, 84(2): 1243-1257. (SARIMA地震季节性分析)

### 3. 空间分析方法

[9] **Silverman B W.** Density estimation for statistics and data analysis[M]. London: CRC press, 1986. (核密度估计经典著作)

[10] **Ord J K, Getis A.** Local spatial autocorrelation statistics: distributional issues and an application[J]. Geographical Analysis, 1995, 27(4): 286-306. (空间自相关分析)

[11] **Anselin L.** Local indicators of spatial association—LISA[J]. Geographical Analysis, 1995, 27(2): 93-115. (LISA方法)

### 4. 板块构造理论

[12] **Morgan W J.** Rises, trenches, great faults, and crustal blocks[J]. Journal of Geophysical Research, 1968, 73(6): 1959-1982. (板块构造理论奠基论文)

[13] **McKenzie D P.** Plate tectonics of the Mediterranean region[J]. Nature, 1970, 226(5242): 239-243. (地中海板块构造)

[14] **Gutenberg B, Richter C F.** Frequency of earthquakes in California[J]. Bulletin of the Seismological Society of America, 1944, 34(4): 185-188. (地震频度-震级关系)

[15] **Wiemer S, Wyss M.** Mapping spatial variability of the frequency-magnitude distribution of earthquakes[J]. Advances in Geophysics, 2002, 45: 259-302. (地震空间分布研究)

### 5. 地震危险性分析

[16] **Cornell C A.** Engineering seismic risk analysis[J]. Bulletin of the Seismological Society of America, 1968, 58(5): 1583-1606. (概率地震危险性分析奠基论文)

- **电子文献**：作者. 题名[EB/OL]. 网址, 访问日期.
